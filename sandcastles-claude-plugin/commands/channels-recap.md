Give me a recap of this channel: $ARGUMENTS

Use the `channel_recap` tool. Pass `$ARGUMENTS` as the channel identifier (UUID, handle, or URL). Synthesize the response into a brief covering the channel's focus, what they've been making content about recently, how they've been performing, and 2-3 specific high-performing example videos with Sandcastles links. If `$ARGUMENTS` is empty, ask the user which channel they want a recap of.
