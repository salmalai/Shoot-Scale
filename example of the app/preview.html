Cheat sheet

How the engine works

Five steps from a new client to a finished batch of scripts. Not sure where to start? Just type /run and it walks you through all of it.

1

Onboard the client  /snapshot

Turns onboarding notes into the client's identity doc — their voice, niche, and hard no-gos.

2

Set their audience  /bullseye

Defines exactly who they're for and how many videos per batch aim at each ring.

3

Analyze their videos  /analyze

Reads what's already working vs. flopping on their page. Uses Sandcastles.

4

Save a winning format  /create-format  optional

Banks a repeatable format so it can be reused for any client later.

5

Produce the batch  /produce

Writes the scripts, grades the hooks, and builds the branded script doc.

The only 3 moments it stops for you

1 · before spending Sandcastles credits  ·  2 · whether a winner becomes a saved format  ·  3 · approving the format split before scripts are written. Everything else runs on its own.
